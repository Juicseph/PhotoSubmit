Timeline
Milestones in Bulldog history
2023
Mountain West Tournament Champions
Fresno State's first MW title and first NCAA Tournament bid since 2002. Dionii Fraga named MW Freshman of the Year.
2012
Joined the Mountain West Conference
Fresno State moved from the WAC to the Mountain West Conference.
2002
23-win season and NCAA Tournament
Kristen Fenton (568 kills) led the program's best modern-era record. Fenton earned All-West Region First Team honors.
1992
Ruth Lawanson wins Olympic bronze
Former Bulldog Ruth Lawanson wins a bronze medal with the US National Team at the XXV Olympiad in Barcelona.
1984
First All-American — Ruth Lawanson
Fresno State's first and only volleyball All-American. Program entered the Top 20 rankings. Lawanson's No. 15 was later retired.
1965
Program founded
Fresno State women's volleyball begins under Coach Gloria Zufall.
